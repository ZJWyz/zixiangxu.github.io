About

I am Zixiang Xu (徐子翔). Now I am a ZJU100 Young Professor (百人计划研究员) School of Mathematical Sciences, Zhejiang University. I was a Postdoc Research fellow (2022.9-2025.8) and Senior Researcher (2025.9-2026.5) at Institute for Basic Science. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in 2022 from the School of Mathematical Sciences, Capital Normal University, supervised by Gennian Ge. In 2016, I received my B.S. degree from the School of Mathematical Science, Zhejiang University.

Research interests

  • Extremal combinatorics
  • Discrete geometry
  • Information theory
  • Building bridges
