I am Zixiang Xu (徐子翔), currently a Senior Researcher in the Extremal Combinatorics and Probability Group at the Institute for Basic Science (IBS), mentored by Hong Liu. I received my Ph.D. in 2022 from the School of Mathematical Sciences, Capital Normal University, under the supervision of Gennian Ge. I received my B.S. in 2016 from Zhejiang University.

My research lies primarily in extremal combinatorics, discrete geometry, and information theory, with a general interest in developing methods that connect different parts of combinatorics.

Research interests

  • Extremal combinatorics
  • Set systems and VC-dimension
  • Discrete geometry and Euclidean Ramsey theory
  • Graph theory and threshold phenomena
  • Information theory and coding theory
