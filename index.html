Zixiang Xu 徐子翔

Senior Researcher, IBS Extremal Combinatorics and Probability Group

I work in extremal combinatorics, discrete geometry, and information theory, with a broad interest in building bridges among different parts of combinatorics. I am currently a Senior Researcher at the Extremal Combinatorics and Probability Group, Institute for Basic Science (IBS), mentored by Hong Liu. I received my Ph.D. in 2022 from the School of Mathematical Sciences, Capital Normal University, under the supervision of Gennian Ge.

In the near future (around summer 2026), I will join Zhejiang University as a Hundred Talents Program Researcher.

About

My research focuses on extremal and structural questions in combinatorics, often with interactions with discrete geometry, graph theory, set systems, coding theory, and probabilistic methods.
